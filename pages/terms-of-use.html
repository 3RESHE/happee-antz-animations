16. Background Check Policy for Job Seekers

By using Happee Antz, job seekers acknowledge and agree to the terms of this Background Check Policy.

16.1 Purpose of Background Checks

The background check process aims to:

  • Verify the accuracy and authenticity of a job seeker’s employment history, qualifications, and references.
  • Assist employers in making informed hiring decisions based on verified information.
  • Maintain a trustworthy job marketplace where job seekers and employers can engage with confidence.

Happee Antz does not conduct background checks on all job seekers by default but may do so under specific circumstances, such as:

  • Upon request from an employer as part of their hiring process.
  • If a job seeker applies for positions requiring higher security, licensing, or professional verification.
  • If inconsistencies are detected in a job seeker’s profile, employment history, or submitted documents.

16.2 Information Verified During Background Checks

As part of the background verification process, Happee Antz may review and confirm the following details:

  • Employment History – Past job roles, companies worked for, and duration of employment.
  • Educational Background – Degrees, certifications, and institutions attended.
  • Professional Licenses & Certifications – Verification of required credentials for specific industries.
  • Character References – Contacting references provided by the job seeker for professional validation.

Important: Background checks will only be conducted using the references and previous employer details provided by the job seeker. Happee Antz will not conduct checks beyond the provided information unless required by law.

16.3 Privacy & Confidentiality of Background Checks

  • Confidentiality: All background check results are strictly confidential and will only be shared with the requesting employer.
  • No Public Disclosure: Background check findings will not be made public or shared with third parties outside the hiring process.
  • Secure Handling of Data: All verification records are processed in compliance with privacy laws and data protection policies.

Job seekers’ personal information will not be misused, sold, or distributed, and all data will be handled with the utmost security and discretion.

16.4 Background Check Appeals & Disputes

Happee Antz recognizes that errors or discrepancies may occur during the background check process. If a job seeker believes their background check results are inaccurate or unfairly assessed, they may:

  • Submit an appeal request to publicrelations@happeeantz.com with the subject “Background Check Appeal”.
  • Provide supporting documentation or evidence to dispute the findings (e.g., employment records, certificates, or reference letters).

Happee Antz will review appeals on a case-by-case basis and provide a resolution within a reasonable timeframe. All decisions regarding background check disputes are final and binding.

16.5 Consent to Background Checks

By applying for jobs on Happee Antz that may require a background check, job seekers:

  • Grant explicit consent to Happee Antz and/or the requesting employer to conduct a background check using the information provided.
  • Acknowledge that refusal to participate in a required background check may result in ineligibility for certain job opportunities.

Job seekers will be notified in advance if a background check is required for a specific job application.

16.6 Limitation of Liability

Happee Antz is not liable for any inaccuracies in background check results caused by incorrect or incomplete information provided by the job seeker. Additionally, Happee Antz does not guarantee employment outcomes based on background check results, as hiring decisions remain the sole discretion of the employer.

16.7 Updates to Background Check Policy

Happee Antz reserves the right to update or modify this Background Check Policy as needed to enhance platform integrity, comply with legal requirements, or improve user experience. Any changes will be reflected in the updated Terms of Use, and users are encouraged to review this policy periodically.
