Effective Date: March 1, 2025

Welcome to Happee Antz ("Company," "we," "our," or "us"). Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting the personal information you share when using our job portal website (www.happeeantz.com). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you access our platform.

By using Happee Antz, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information from job seekers, employers, and visitors to improve our services:

1.1 Personal Information

When you sign up for an account or use our services, we may collect:

  • Job Seekers: Name, email address, phone number, resume/CV, skills, work experience, educational background, and profile photo.
  • Employers: Name, company name, business email, company logo, job postings, and employer profile details.
  • Payment Information: If you purchase a premium service, we collect payment details, but all transactions are securely processed by our third-party payment providers.

1.2 Non-Personal Information

We automatically collect certain data when you visit our website, including:

  • IP address and browser type
  • Device information and operating system
  • Usage statistics and browsing behavior
  • Cookies and tracking technologies (see Cookies Policy below)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our job portal services
  • To match job seekers with relevant job opportunities
  • To allow employers to post and manage job listings
  • To process transactions and send payment confirmations
  • To communicate updates, job alerts, and platform announcements
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent activities
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. However, we may share your data with:

  • Employers: If you apply for a job, your information (resume, contact details, etc.) is shared with the employer.
  • Job Seekers: Employers may view your public profile if you apply to their listings.
  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors (e.g., payment processors, cloud hosting) that help us operate the platform.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law to protect against fraud, security threats, or unlawful activities.

4. Data Security & Retention

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of data transmission is 100% secure. Your data will be retained as long as your account is active or as needed to comply with legal obligations. If you wish to delete your account, you may request removal by contacting us at publicrelations@happeeantz.com.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking tools to enhance user experience, analyze website traffic, and personalize content. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

Types of Cookies We Use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for platform functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Helps us understand user behavior
  • Marketing Cookies: Personalizes job recommendations and ads

To exercise these rights, please email us at publicrelations@happeeantz.com.
